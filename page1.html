INTRODUCTION TO ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

What is Artificial Intelligence ?

Artificial Intelligence ( AI) is a wide-ranging computer science branch dedicated to developing smart machines that can perform tasks that usually involve human intelligence. AI is an interdisciplinary science with multiple approaches, but advancements in machine learning and deep learning are creating a paradigm shift in virtually every sector of the tech industry.
